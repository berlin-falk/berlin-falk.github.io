Charlottenborg Art Talks: Superflex

The artist trio Superflex will visit the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts for a public, inspirational talk on February 21st at 17:00, at the Academy's Festsal.
 
The subjects of the talk will be artistic strategy, the artist groups' current exhibition One Two Three Swing! at Tate Modern and other relevant subjects, developed in conversation with Kunsthal Charlottenborg’s director Michael Thouber.
 
The talk is part of the Charlottenborg Art Talk program and it is arranged in a collaboration between the The Royal Danish Academy, academy student George Koutsouris (School of Media Arts) and Kunsthal Charlottenborg.

Charlottenborg Art Talk: Superflex
At The Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts, Schools of Visual Arts, Festsalen, Kongens Nytorv 1
Free admission.

NB: The talk will be filmed
