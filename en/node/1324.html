The Exhibition Space Q

Address

Peder Skrams Gade 2
1050 Kbh. K

Info

For information about current exhibitions, see Current events and visit our Facebook page

Q is the students’ own exhibition venue. It is operated by and for students through the Student Council

Q offers an open, all-embracing space where students can experiment with a range of exhibition formats, collaborative approaches, workshops, performances and different modes of representation.

Each year, the first-year students will present their work in Q. A firmly established and much-loved tradition, this exhibition usually takes place in February. The first-year students’ exhibition is an important part of their education, not least because they handle all aspects themselves: planning, installation and presentation/communication.
