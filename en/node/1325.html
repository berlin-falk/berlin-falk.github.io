Afgang (MFA Degree Show)

Afgang (the MFA Degree Show) is the most important annual event for the Schools of Visual Arts. Here, graduates conclude six years of study by presenting their final projects as part of a joint public exhibition  

Afgang serves a dual purpose: it is a public exhibition and the final exam for MFA graduates. The Afgang event has been held at Kunsthal Charlottenborg since 2013. The students themselves choose the person(s) responsible for co-ordinating their MFA Degree Show, including the work associated with installing the exhibition, preparing the catalogue and so on.

From EXIT to Afgang

From 1997 to 2009 the MFA Degree Show was presented at Kunstforeningen Gl Strand. Known as EXIT, this annual exhibition gradually became something of an institution in its own right. 
In 2010 the MFA Degree Show was relocated to Charlottenborg and changed its name to Afgang. 
In 2011 and 2012 the vaulted spaces of Nikolaj Kunsthal, a former church, formed the setting for the exhibition.
In 2013 the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts merged with Kunsthal Charlottenborg, prompting the MFA Degree Show to once again move into its current home: Kunsthal Charlottenborg.
