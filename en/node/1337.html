The History of Schools of Visual Arts

On this page you can read about the history of the Schools of Visual Arts. The history goes back to the foundation of Det Kongelig Danske Skildre-, Bildhugger- og Bygnings-Academi i Kiøbenhavn in 1754. A story that has been marked by great changes as well as a continuous focus on the core task:
to educate artists
