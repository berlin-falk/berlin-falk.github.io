GO TO The Figura series

Visual archiving

Visual archiving is focusing mainly on recording and publishing student works. The series puts its main emphasis on the annual open-house exhibitions Rundgang and on the students’ ventures abroad. The visual archiving series also publishes classics, field work and other works of interest.

Homo Sacer afsluttet og genåbnet
