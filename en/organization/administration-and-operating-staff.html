Administration and operating staff

Jes Gjørup

Acting rector, Director of Administration and Finance

Referring to the rector, the director of administration and finance holds the overall responsibility for the organization’s administration. The director of administration and finance is part of the top management of the organization alongside the rector, prorector and the director of Kunsthal Charlottenborg.
The director of administration and finance also holds overall HR responsibility for all technical and administrative staff within the secretariat, the study administration and the facility service unit. The main tasks undertaken by the director of administration and finance include: finances, annual reporting, performance contract compliance and management, HR and salary administration, legal matters and facility services.

Allan Rene Meincke

Operations manager

The chief tasks of the Operations department comprise two main areas: access control and facility services. Access control involves all matters associated with alarms, locks, keys and keycards. Facility services covers a range of regular tasks involving technical installations, janitorial services and minor interior maintenance.
Operations staff also carry out a number of other tasks associated with e.g. student enrolment, the Rundgang open house events, in-house relocations and reallocations, and clearing up over the summer.
