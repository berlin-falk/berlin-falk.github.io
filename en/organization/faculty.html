The International Center for Knowledge in the Arts

The Center is founded by the Danish institutions of higher arts education and anchored at the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts, Copenhagen.

Member institutions: The Royal Danish Academy of Music, Rhythmic Music Conservatory, The Royal Academy of Music, Danish National Academy of Music, National Film School of Denmark, The Danish National School of Performing Arts, The Royal Danish Academy of Arts.
